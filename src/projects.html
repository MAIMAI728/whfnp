Jaspals Voice

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AWS

Susan Graham was inspired to create Jaspal's Voice after her sister contracted Motor Neurone Disease and subsequently lost her voice to the disease. Jaspal's Voice aims to collate and review communication devices for those suffering from MND, by providing a catalogue of devices with user-generated reviews and tips.

Volunteers built the first version of the Jaspal's Voice website in a few short weeks, using Java, the Play framework, and Amazon Web Services. The team​ has also been hard at work on an Android app (soon to be launched) that will provide an easy means of communication and education for people afflicted with or interested in learning more about the disease.

Pimp My Cause

Django, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Heroku

Pimp My Cause connects marketers, researchers, and innovators who wish to volunteer to good causes in need of innovative solutions. The organisation has successfully matchmade over 1,300 charities and volunteers since its inception in 2011.

WHFNP volunteers are tackling a website rebuild project for this charity to modernise the technology stack and improve the site's user experience. Using Django, Sass and JavaScript, the end goal is to leave Pimp My Cause with a slick new website that is robust, sustainable, and capable of supporting the growth in its membership expected over the next few years.

Together in the UK Project

Wordpress, CSS, HTML

TGIUK is a space for people who have come from outside the UK to live and work here. A space where people can share their insights and stories and engage with others about their future.

The organisation aims to help those new to the UK to settle in and become more familiar with the UK culture and customs. The project is to help TGIUK with design of a professional site that will clearly show their purpose, be inviting and create momentum.
